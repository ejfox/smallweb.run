# How to write slides

Split pages by horizontal ruler (`---`). It's very simple!

```markdown
# Slide 1

foobar

---

# Slide 2

foobar

---
# Smallweb
Smallweb is a thing, in a place, made by a person, in time...  
trippy...  
Check out the link on slide one ;)

---
```
